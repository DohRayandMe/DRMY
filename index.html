Three daughters.
Degrees. Careers. Lives beautifully lived.

All three diagnosed — ADD, ADHD, Personality Disorder.

For years I watched them search for their balance. I searched alongside them.

And somewhere in that search — I found mine.
I was undiagnosed my whole life.
It was my daughters who showed me who I was. To find me, I had to meet you. I found me because of them. That is why Doh Ray & Me exists.
Every child deserves to find their Me. 💛
